BITE

BITE is an initiative that rewards you for biking, with weekly, monthly and yearly prizes. The purpose of this initiative is to fight climate change and solve the increasing problem of air-quality. We are currently located in Oslo, Trondheim and Bergen, although we plan on expanding to other cities and countries.

Connect your BITE-account with your Oslo-, Trondheim- or Bergen-bysykkel account to start collecting your kilometers. Check out “rewards” for the different prizes.

The app

The BITE app that connects to your bike and track how far you ride.
This app is one step further to stop climate changes! The goal is to adapt and reduce the consequences of climate change by cycling more. With the app, you can get special rewards such as discounts just by cycling!

Download our app “BITE” to connect with other parties, check your statistics and collect your rewards. The app can be downloaded from Apple app store or google play store.

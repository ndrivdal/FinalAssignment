BITE

Weekly rewards

Bite is an initiative that rewards you for biking, with weekly, monthly and yearly prizes. The purpose of this initiative is to fight climate change and solve the increasing problem of air-quality. We are currently only located in Oslo, Trondheim and Bergen, although we plan on expanding to other cities and countries.

Connect your BITE-account with your Oslo-, Trondheim- or Bergen-bysykkel account to start collecting your kilometers. Check out “rewards” for our the different prizes.
